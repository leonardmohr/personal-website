---
title: Apple-Ginger Green Smoothie
description: A fresh green smoothie with kale, apple, and ginger.
---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup packed baby kale, curly green kale (tough ribs removed), or spinach
- ½ medium banana, cut into 1-inch chunks
- ½ Granny Smith apple, cored and roughly chopped
- ½-inch knob of fresh ginger, peeled
- Small handful of fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon hemp seeds or chia seeds (optional)
- ¾ to 1 cup water
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Several ice cubes

## Instructions

1. Add the kale, banana, apple, ginger, and cilantro to a blender. Add hemp or chia seeds if using.
2. Pour in the water and lemon juice. Add the ice cubes.
3. Blend on high until completely smooth.
4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

Makes 2 cups.
