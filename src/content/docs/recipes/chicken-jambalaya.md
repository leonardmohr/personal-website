---
title: Chicken Jambalaya
description: A quick low-fat chicken jambalaya.
---

## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ cup chopped green sweet pepper
- 2 tablespoons margarine or butter
- 1 can (14½ oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1½ cups chicken broth
- ½ cup long grain rice
- 1 teaspoon dried basil or thyme, crushed
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- ¼ to ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet, cook the celery, onion, and sweet pepper in margarine or butter over medium heat until the vegetables are tender but not browned.
2. Stir in the undrained tomatoes, chicken broth, uncooked rice, basil or thyme, garlic powder, pepper, hot pepper sauce, and bay leaf.
3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
4. Stir in the chicken or turkey and heat through. Discard the bay leaf before serving.

Makes 4 servings.
