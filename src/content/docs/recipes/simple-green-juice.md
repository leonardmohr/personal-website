---
title: Simple Green Juice
description: A 6-ingredient fresh green juice made with kale, apple, ginger, and cucumber.
---

## Ingredients

- 1 bunch kale (about 5 oz)
- 1-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled
- 1 Granny Smith apple (or any large apple)
- 5 celery stalks, ends trimmed
- ½ large English cucumber
- Handful of fresh parsley (about 1 oz)

## Instructions

1. Wash and prep the vegetables, cutting them into large chunks.
2. **If using a juicer:** juice the ingredients in the order listed. Pour into glasses and enjoy immediately.
3. **If using a blender:** cut everything into smaller pieces (remove tough kale veins). Blend on high until smooth. Pour through a fine mesh sieve, pressing the pulp with the back of a spoon to extract as much liquid as possible. Pour into glasses and enjoy.

Makes 2 large glasses.
