---
title: Lentil Soup
description: A simple, hearty lentil soup from Beth Mohr.
---

## Ingredients

- ¾ cup dry lentils (red or other), rinsed and drained
- 1 cup chopped onion (½ large onion or 1 small)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 cup sliced carrots (about 2 medium)
- ½ cup or more celery, green pepper, or red pepper (optional)
- 2 teaspoons Better Than Bouillon (beef)
- 5 cups water
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- ¼ to ½ teaspoon smoked paprika

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add the onion, carrots, and any other vegetables. Cook, stirring occasionally, until transparent and softened.
3. Add the garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
4. Add the smoked paprika and cook for another minute.
5. Add the water, lentils, Better Than Bouillon, and Italian seasoning. Stir to combine.
6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20–30 minutes, or until the lentils and vegetables are tender. Red lentils cook faster than green or brown lentils.

Use ½ to 1 cup of lentils depending on how thick you like the soup.
