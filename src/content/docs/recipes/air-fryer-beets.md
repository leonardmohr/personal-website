---
title: Air Fryer Beets
description: Crispy golden outsides, sweet and tender insides — ready in under 30 minutes.
---

## Ingredients

- 3 medium beets
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Peel and slice each beet into 8 pieces. In a mixing bowl, toss the beets with the olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Transfer the beets to the air fryer basket in a single layer (work in batches if needed). Set the temperature to 390°F (200°C) and cook for 18–20 minutes, or until fork tender.
3. Remove from the air fryer and serve.

Serves 4.
